Abinaya Sridharan

PhD Scholar, Biological Sciences and Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India

Research Summary: I am a PhD student working on developing a CRISPR/Cas-driven electrochemical biosensor for detecting drug-resistant genes using miniaturised lab-made electrodes. Motivated by a strong interest in interdisciplinary research, I integrate concepts from molecular biology, materials science, and analytical chemistry to advance biosensor technologies.

I am actively seeking international collaboration to explore the integration of CRISPR/Cas systems with electrochemical biosensors for innovative healthcare and environmental applications.

Core Interests:

  •   CRISPR–Cas & Aptamer-based Biosensors
  •   Electrochemical Signal Transduction
  •   Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) Detection
  •   Nanomaterial-engineered Electrodes

Research Projects

CRISPR-Cas Driven Electrochemical Biosensors

PhD Thesis | IIT Gandhinagar | July 2024 - Present

  • Developing a novel diagnostic platform utilising CRISPR/Cas systems for the highly specific detection of drug-resistant genes.
  • Designing miniaturised lab-made electrodes to enhance portability and point-of-care utility.
  • Integrating nanomaterial-engineered surfaces to improve electrochemical signal transduction and sensitivity.

    • Enzymatic Production of Prebiotic Oligosaccharides

    M.Tech Thesis | Mepco Schlenk Engineering College | 2022 - 2024

  • Focused on the enzymatic hydrolysis of fenugreek gum using Endo-1,4-β-mannanase derived from Bacillus sp.
  • Optimized production parameters to yield galactomannan oligosaccharides.
  • Evaluated the prebiotic potential of the produced oligosaccharides for nutraceutical applications.

    • Nanophotocatalyst Grafted Membranes

    B.Tech Thesis | NIT Trichy & VCW | 2020

  • Grafted catalysts onto PVDF membranes for simultaneous filtration and degradation of pollutants.
  • Successfully demonstrated degradation of Tetracycline (antibiotic) and Humic acid in wastewater samples.
